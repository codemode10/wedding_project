Lourmarin

Listed as "one of the most beautiful villages in France", Lourmarin nestles in the middle of vineyards, olive groves and almond trees. Lourmarin has been settled for at least a thousand years, and was a Neolithic campsite before that. A fortress was first built at the site in the 12th century, and was rebuilt by Foulques d'Agoult in the 15th century on the foundations of the earlier castle.

Aix-en-Provence is the capital of the region and a university town in the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region of southern France. It was the birthplace of Post-Impressionist painter Paul Cézanne. Saturday markets here go off.

Marseille

Marseille, is Frances 2nd largest city. It is a port city in southern France and has been a crossroads of immigration and trade since its founding by the Greeks circa 600 B.C. The best two areas are Le Panier and Va. Le Panier is a beautiful old artsy area with great resturants and bars. Vallon des Auffes is an amazing place to go for a sunset drink or meal.

About French Weddings. Weddings in Frace are sligtly different to those in Australia. In France you can only legally be married in your local the town hall. Some couples chose to do this part before the wedding day and some chose to do this the morning of the wedding day with just a few people in attendence. France is quite a religious coutry and marriage celebrants arnt much of a thing as they hold no legal rights. So we chose to do the town hall part last year with the wedding this September.
