About French Weddings

Weddings in France are slightly different to those in Australia. In France you can only legally be married in your local town hall. Some couples chose to do this part before the wedding day and some chose to do this the morning of the wedding day with just a few people in attendance. France is quite a religious country and marriage celebrants are not common as they hold no legal rights. So we chose to do the town hall part last year with the wedding this September.
